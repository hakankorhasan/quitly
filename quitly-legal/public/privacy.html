For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the UK, or other regions with applicable privacy laws, we request your consent before collecting or processing data for advertising purposes. You may update your consent preferences at any time via the app's Settings screen.

Quitly is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Data Retention

Your on-device data remains until you delete the app. iCloud data is managed through your Apple ID settings. Crash logs retained by Firebase are automatically deleted after 90 days. RevenueCat purchase records are retained as required by applicable law.
