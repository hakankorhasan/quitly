Privacy Policy

👋 Introduction

Welcome to Soberli ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

By using Soberli, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

📊 Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Habit tracking data (quit date, streak information)
  • Daily mood check-in entries
  • Reward goals and progress
  • App preferences and settings
  • Daily cost and currency preferences

Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics (provided by Google) to collect anonymous usage data to improve the app experience. This may include:

  • App usage patterns and session duration
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Country/region (not precise location)

This data is collected in an aggregated, anonymized form and cannot be used to personally identify you.

Subscription Data

If you subscribe to Soberli Pro, your purchase is processed by Apple's App Store and managed through RevenueCat. We receive:

  • An anonymous subscriber ID
  • Subscription status (active/expired)
  • Purchase date and expiration date

We do not receive your name, email, payment method, or any other personally identifiable information through this process.

🔒 How We Protect Your Data

  • All personal habit data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework
  • We do not operate user accounts or require registration
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties
  • Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated
  • Widget data is shared only between the app and its widget extension using Apple's secure App Groups

🤝 Third-Party Services

Soberli uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics – Anonymous usage analytics. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Remote Config – App configuration delivery. No personal data is collected.
  • RevenueCat – Subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit – In-app purchase processing, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy

👶 Children's Privacy

Soberli is not intended for use by children under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

🗑️ Data Deletion

Since your habit data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it:

  • Use the "Reset All Data" option in Settings to delete all app data
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data
  • To request deletion of analytics data, contact us at the email below

✏️ Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date below. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
